What We Do

Specialist Mold Removal Services

From poorly ventilated rooms to deep structural damp, we treat mold at its source — not just on the surface.

Ventilation Mold

Mold caused by poor airflow and excess humidity. We address the root cause — inadequate ventilation — to deliver lasting results and protect indoor air quality.

Structural Mold

Moisture that has penetrated walls, floors, and ceilings. We treat hidden mold in bathrooms, behind cupboards, cracked walls, and poorly constructed spaces before it causes lasting damage.

Common Mistakes

Why Painting Over Mold Makes It Worse

It's a common shortcut. Here's why it always fails — and why proper removal is the only real solution.

It Only Hides the Problem

Paint covers the stain but leaves mold roots and spores fully intact beneath the surface — still active, still spreading.

Mold Continues to Grow Beneath

Even mold-resistant paint fails when the moisture source is still present. The colony keeps growing, unseen.

Traps Moisture, Accelerates Spread

Sealing mold under paint creates a warm, damp environment — ideal for further growth into walls, floors, and ceilings.

Delays Treatment, Increases Cost

Every week you wait, the mold spreads deeper. What could have been a straightforward fix becomes an extensive repair.

A Legal and Liability Risk

Concealing mold — particularly in rental or commercial properties — can constitute negligence and expose landlords and businesses to serious liability.
